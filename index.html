04 — Yours alone

Everything stays on your device.

No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. No subscriptions. Your projects never leave your machine — and no one but you can open them.

✕ accounts

No sign-up

Open the app and start. No email, no password, no wall.

✕ cloud

No servers

Files live locally in your browser, not on someone’s database.

✕ tracking

No watching

No analytics following you around. We can’t see your work.

✕ fees

No subscription

Make as many zines as you want. Nothing to cancel.

✕ lock-in

No access

No one can reach your projects — not even us.

✓ ownership

Your work is yours

Export anytime. It belongs to you, full stop.
